# Product Vision

## Product definition

Elev8 OS is a WordPress-based operating system for creative studios, art schools, makerspaces, and instructor-led class businesses.

It is designed to sit above systems such as Amelia and WooCommerce, combining operational data into workflows that are specific to creative studios.

## Primary users

### Owner

Needs a fast understanding of revenue, classes, demand, payouts, profitability, customer activity, and business health.

### Manager

Needs clear operational tasks, artist information, customer issues, booking visibility, and reporting.

### Elev8 Member Artist

Needs access to classes, schedules, earnings, payouts, referrals, documents, profile information, public links, and communication.

### Customer

Needs simple discovery, booking, waitlist access, artist information, payment options, and follow-up communication.

### Bookkeeper

Needs accurate payout history, financial exports, artist documents, and reconciliation support.

## Elev8 Brain

Elev8 Brain is the future recommendation layer.

It should not exist merely to chat. It should notice patterns and surface actions, such as:

- A class is nearly full and may justify another session.
- A class is under-filled and needs promotion.
- Students have not returned in a set number of days.
- Artist payouts are due.
- A supply is likely to run out.
- A class is producing revenue but little or no profit.
- A waitlist indicates unmet demand.

## Product boundaries

Elev8 OS should not rebuild functions that Amelia, WooCommerce, WordPress, or accounting systems already perform well unless integration is unreliable or the studio-specific workflow requires direct control.

Elev8 OS should become the decision and workflow layer, not duplicate every external platform.
