Elev8 OS 7.2.1 — Artist Sale Notifications

- Added a reusable Asset Engine sale-notification service.
- Sends the owning artist a sale email after WooCommerce confirms payment.
- Includes artwork image, title, asset number, sale price, order number, date, and sold status.
- Uses the shared Notification Service and preserves WooCommerce customer receipts.
- Prevents duplicate artist emails across Processing and Completed order transitions.
- Records delivery results in private WooCommerce order notes.
- Alerts the site administrator when the artist has no valid WordPress email.
- Preserves all existing Asset Engine, inventory, WooCommerce, artist, and live data.
