Elev8 OS 9.1.0 Installation

1. Install elev8-os-9.1.0-artist-growth-center-foundation.zip on Local.
2. Activate or update Elev8 OS.
3. Sign in as a mapped artist.
4. Open Artist Dashboard and verify the Your Students tickler.
5. Open Students and verify lifetime relationships, segments, search, tags, notes, and timeline.
6. Test that one artist cannot open another artist's relationship records.
7. Install the exact same ZIP on Live only after Local testing passes.

Amelia remains the source of truth for bookings and customer identity. Elev8 OS stores only artist-owned CRM enrichment such as notes and tags.
