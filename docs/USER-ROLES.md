# User Roles and Permissions

## Administrator

Can manage all settings, artists, partnerships, payouts, documents, reports, customers, and development records.

## Owner

Future dedicated role with full business visibility but without unrelated WordPress administration.

## Manager

Can manage artists, classes, customer workflows, and operational reports as explicitly permitted.

## Elev8 Member Artist

Can access only their own:

- Profile
- Public-page settings
- Classes
- Students where legally and operationally appropriate
- Earnings
- Payouts
- Referrals
- Documents
- Schedule
- Messages

An artist must never access another artist's private records.

## Bookkeeper

Can access financial reports, payout records, and required tax-document status without broad website-control permissions.

## Marketing

Can access approved public profile content, classes, referral campaigns, and marketing reports without private financial or tax data.

## Customer

Can access public content and their own future customer-account information.

## Principle of least privilege

Every role should see only what is needed to perform its work.
